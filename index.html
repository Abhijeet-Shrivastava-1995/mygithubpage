FoxoGyan - The Mozillian Club

This club was initially a Firefox Student Ambassadors club which was founded by Mr. Deepak Jain and was inaugurated on 28th January 2014 in the campus of Gyan Ganga Institute of Technology and Sciences. The club organised grand events which gained global recognition from the worldwide mozillian community and brought a good nama for the institute as well. Later in 2016, the ClubLead's responsibilty was handed down to Abhijeet Shrivastava for some time. But due to some changes in the community policies, the Firefox Student Ambassadors programme was suspended. But the journey didnt stopped there, and the then clublead applied for a bigger level, to gain an approval for being a Mozilla Club, making the current clublead the first Mozilla Club Captain.

Core Members

Who We Are

FoxoGyan members are a group of creative student ambassadors leading events and projects in the college to contribute to mozilla.They encourage students to contribute to mozilla's mission and utilize its products.They strive to educate everyone about the power of WEB.

What We Do

As a part of FoxoGyan,we promote firefox for desktop and android,educate other's about mozilla's mission.The role of a FoxoGyan member is to help in technical development of the society and to promote openess,innovation and awareness about the WEB to students.

Global Services

Members of FOXOGYAN have hosted successful events such as TECHOUR in which they have taught students of two different schools so far, about the advantages of using mozila and its various tools.They are on a mission to further conduct bigger events and thus promote the club.
